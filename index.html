Normal Doors

Oxford languages states that a door is, "a hinged, sliding, or revolving barrier at the entrance to a building, room, or vehicle, or in the framework of a cupboard."

There are around 2.3 billion houses with an average of 17.5 doors per house. This makes it so there are 40250000000 doors from houses alone.

Normal Wheels

Wheels are defined by oxford languages as, "a circular object that revolves on an axle and is fixed below a vehicle or other object to enable it to move easily over the ground."

There are around 1.474 billion cars in the world with 5 wheels (including steering wheel) each, which totals to 5.895 billion wheels from cars alone.
