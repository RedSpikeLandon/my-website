Habitat/Development

Orangutans mainly live at rainforests in Indonesia and Malaysia. However, orangutan can also be found in areas of Borneo and Sumatra.

Female orangutans weigh near 82 pounds on average and grow to around 45 inches in height.

Male orangutans weigh near 191 pounds on average and grow to around 54 inches in height.

Fun facts

Wheels are defined by oxford languages as, "a circular object that revolves on an axle and is fixed below a vehicle or other object to enable it to move easily over the ground."

There are around 1.474 billion cars in the world with 5 wheels (including steering wheel) each, which totals to 5.895 billion wheels from cars alone.
